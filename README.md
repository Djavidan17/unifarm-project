# unifarm-project

About ℹ

Unifarm is a digital platform designed to support farmers by providing a single ecosystem for agricultural business operations.

The platform aims to connect farmers, buyers, and agricultural partners in one place, allowing them to trade products, establish contracts, and access important information related to agriculture.

Our goal is to simplify communication within the agricultural sector and provide farmers with modern digital tools for managing and expanding their business.

Core Features ⚙

Marketplace for buying and selling agricultural products

Contract system between farmers and partners

Access to agricultural news and information

Digital tools to support farm management and business operations

Vision 🌱

Unifarm is intended to become a national agricultural hub where farmers can interact, collaborate, and grow their businesses through a unified digital platform.
